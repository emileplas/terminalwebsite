About me:

I am a full stack developer based in Belgium and Brazil. From a young age I have always been interested in creating with computers. I started building websites and forums at the age of 14 using HTML and CSS. After high school I took a small non-technological detour. I graduated from law school and was active as a real estate and environemntal lawyer for 4 years working with various clients.

Missing the creative process, I decided to recenter my attention back on developping. While being a lawyer I took several courses (python, java and android development) and played around with my raspberry pi. Leaving the bar association in the summer of 2021, I enrolled in the Codecademy full stack developer course. In february 2022 I enrolled myself for part-time bachelor of computer science.

Currently I am working on various projects. Type projects to discover what I have been up to lately.
